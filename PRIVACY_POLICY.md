# Privacy Policy for kairo

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2024

## Overview

kairo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Birth Information**: Date, time, and location of birth (city, country, coordinates)
- This information is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to our servers

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: We do not collect any device information
- **Usage Data**: We do not track or collect any usage analytics
- **Location Data**: Location is only used during onboarding to help you select your birth location. We do not track your current location or collect ongoing location data

## How We Use Your Information

We use your birth information solely to:

- Calculate your personalized astrological birth chart
- Generate daily, weekly, and monthly horoscope insights
- Provide astrological cycle information

## Data Storage

All of your personal information is stored **locally on your device** using iOS's secure UserDefaults system. We do not:

- Upload your data to any servers
- Store your data in any cloud services
- Share your data with any third parties
- Track or analyze your usage

## Data Security

Your birth information is protected by:

- iOS device encryption
- Local storage only (never transmitted over the internet)
- No external database or server connections

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Delete Your Data**: You can delete all your birth information from the app's profile settings at any time
- **Control Your Data**: Your data never leaves your device, giving you complete control

## Third-Party Services

kairo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline using locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

kairo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. Since kairo stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit any data to servers, we do not "sell" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:

- We are not a data controller or processor as we do not collect or transmit your data
- All data remains on your device under your control
- You can delete all data at any time from the app settings

---

**Summary**: kairo is a privacy-first application. Your birth information stays on your device, is never uploaded to any servers, and is used solely to provide you with personalized astrological insights.
